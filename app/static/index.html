If you want to sync private playlists (like your YouTube Music Liked Songs) or if you are running into YouTube rate-limits (HTTP 403 Forbidden errors), follow these steps to export long-lived cookies:
1. Install a browser extension such as Get cookies.txt LOCALLY in Chrome/Firefox (ensure it is allowed to run in Incognito).
2. Open a new Incognito / Private Window in your browser.
3. Visit music.youtube.com and sign in.
4. Navigate directly to youtube.com/robots.txt (visiting this static page minimizes cookie rotation).
5. Open the extension icon, download your cookies as a text file, and upload it above.
6. Close the Incognito window immediately without browsing YouTube further in that session.
